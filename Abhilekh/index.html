About Us

Abhilekh- A Design & Build Initiative

Welcome to Abhilekhâ€”the centralized repository of Shapoorji Pallonji Private Limited's rich legacy of projects. Designed to bring together scattered project files under one unified platform, Abhilekh serves as a treasure trove of knowledge, innovation, and experience, encompassing the diverse fields of Aviation, Healthcare, Industrial, Residential, Water, Commercial, Hospitality, Institutions, and Sports Arenas.

This initiative is a step towards fostering collaboration, efficiency, and continuous improvement. By offering easy access to comprehensive project overviews, Abhilekh enables engineers and teams to learn from the best practices, innovative solutions, and lessons gained from completed projects. It acts as a guide to navigate challenges, mitigate risks, and implement successful strategies in future endeavors.

Previously, project data was dispersed across regional offices and sites, creating barriers to knowledge sharing. With Abhilekh, all employees can seamlessly access essential project-related information, ensuring that our collective expertise drives every new project forward.

Join us in leveraging the power of our shared history to build a stronger, smarter future with Abhilekh.
